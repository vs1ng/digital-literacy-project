Stay Safe Online: Student Awareness Guide


Enable 2FA: Always use Two-Factor Authentication on your college email and social media accounts.


UPI Safety (Financial): Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; a PIN is only required to send or pay.


Verify Links: Do not click on suspicious links in SMS or WhatsApp claiming you have won a prize or your account is blocked.


Secure Passwords: Use a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols for all portal logins.


QR Code Caution (Financial): Never scan a QR code sent by someone claiming to pay you; scanning a code deducts money from your account.


Public Wi-Fi: Avoid accessing bank accounts or sensitive student portals while connected to unsecured public Wi-Fi.


Check Permissions: Regularly review app permissions on your phone to ensure apps aren't accessing your mic or camera unnecessarily.


Official Reporting: If you are a victim of a scam, report it immediately at cybercrime.gov.in or call the National Helpline at 1930.
